dungeoneer icon

I conceptualized this animation for a Discord server Dungeoneer, dedicated to running exalt dungeons from Realm of the Mad God. The icon incorporates bosses from these dungeons, including Oryx the Mad God 3, Factory Control Core, The Forgotten King, Malus, Marble Colossus, and the Void Entity.

Similar to my Shatters Icon, I based my animations off of in-game sprite work. The transitions between the bosses combines some animations present in the game with some of my own ideas. Unlike the other icon, though, I utilized Adobe Animate (I gained some experience with it during my class during fall 2022). However, I did still use Photoshop for the primary illustrations; while a bit cumbersome, I feel more comfortable doing more detailed illustrations in Photoshop over Animate.
