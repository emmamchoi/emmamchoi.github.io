knight goes in for the stun

I illustrated this mock manga page for a Realm of the Mad God Discord competition. The concept for this scene was that the knight catches the final boss, Oryx the Mad God 3, off guard by asking him out instead of trying to defeat him. The title references the knight's ability to stun. I did this project in black-and-white because most printed mangas are. The backgrounds were a bit of a depature from what I usually do, as I attempted to emulate some textures that appear in some manga pages.
