shatters icon

I conceptualized this animation for a Discord server dedicated to the Realm of the Mad God dungeon, The Shatters. The initial design was for a winter holiday-themed icon competition, hence the Santa hat. The basic idea for this icon was a transition phase of The Forgotten King.

Basing my own illustrations off of the official sprite work, I made this sequence of images into a gif. Since this would be used for an icon of size 512x512 px, I went for a simpler, sketchy style, since super small details wouldn't matter too much in terms of the visibility. I made a few backgrounds on my own, choosing a red/orange chaotic look to reflect the despair/agony I feel is represented in this sequence.

Adobe Photoshop, After Effects; December 2021
